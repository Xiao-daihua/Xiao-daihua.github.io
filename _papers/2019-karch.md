abstract: "We describe a web of well-known dualities connecting quantum field theories in \\$d=1+1\\$ dimensions. The web is constructed by gauging \\$\\\\textbackslash bf Z\\\\_2\\$ global symmetries and includes a number of perennial favourites such as the Jordan-Wigner transformation, Kramers-Wannier duality, bosonization of a Dirac fermion, and T-duality. There are also less-loved examples, such as non-modular invariant \\$c=1\\$ CFTs that depend on a background spin structure."
