abstract:
  This book provides a comprehensive introduction to boundary conformal field theory and its application to D-branes in string theory. It develops the worldsheet approach to D-branes, explaining how boundary conditions in conformal field theory encode the physics of open strings and D-branes. The authors present both the algebraic and geometric aspects, including the role of modular invariance, Cardy conditions, and boundary states. Applications to string compactifications and the classification of consistent boundary conditions are discussed in detail, making the book a standard reference for researchers in conformal field theory and string theory.
