abstract: "We show that there is a fermionic minimal model, i.e. a 1+1d conformal field theory which contains operators of half-integral spins in its spectrum, for each \\$c=1-6/m(m+1)\\$, \\$m\\textbackslash ge 3\\$. This generalizes the Majorana fermion for \\$c=1/2\\$, \\$m=3\\$ and the smallest \\$\\textbackslash mathcal\\N\\\\=\\1\\$ supersymmetric minimal model for \\$c=7/10\\$, \\$m=4\\$. We provide explicit Hamiltonians on Majorana chains realizing these fermionic minimal models."
