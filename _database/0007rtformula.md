## Original Papers

In 2006, it was proposed by Ryu and Takayanagi that the entanglement of a boundary region in a holographic theory can be computed by the area of a minimal surface in the bulk that is homologous to the boundary region. This formula is now known as the Ryu-Takayanagi formula.

- [Holographic Derivation of Entanglement Entropy from AdS/CFT](/papers/2006-ryu/) Ryu and Takayanagi's original paper on the Ryu-Takayanagi formula.



## Proofs

Proofs based on some assumptions were given by Maldacena, and later by Dong.

- [The Gravity Dual of Renyi Entropy](/papers/2016-dong/) Dong's paper on the proof of the Ryu-Takayanagi formula.
