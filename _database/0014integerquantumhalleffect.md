## Original Papers

Integer quantum hall effect was first observed experimentally. The original theoreitical explanation was given by Laughlin.

- [Quantized Hall conductivity in two dimensions](/papers/1981-laughlin/) Laughlin's original paper on IQHE.

The idea of boundary excitation was emphasized by Halperin.

- [Quantized Hall Conductance, Current-Carrying Edge States, and the Existence of Extended States in a Two-Dimensional Disordered Potential](/papers/1982-halperin/) Halperin's original paper on IQHE.
