## Moore Seiberg Construction of RCFT

A series of extremely important and inspiring papers by Moore and Seiberg reveals a deep mathematical structure underlying rational conformal field theories (RCFTs). 

The main idea is that the conformal blocks of a RCFT forms a vector space, and represent the so called Moore-Seiberg groupoid. These conformal blocks satisfy a set of duality relations and the duality matircies satisfy a set of consistency conditions. 

By solving the consistency conditions, one can determine the duality matrices, which characterize a lot of information in RCFT. 

Moreover, the conformal blocks also give rise to a mathematical structure called modular tensor category, which is a very important mathematical structure in the study of TQFT, topological order ...


## Original Papers 

- [Classical and quantum conformal field theory](/papers/1989-moore/) by Moore and Seiberg, 1989. One of the most important papers in the field.
- [Taming the conformal zoo](/papers/1989-moore2/) by Moore and Seiberg, 1989. 
- [Naturality in conformal field theory](/papers/1989-moore3/) by Moore and Seiberg, 1989.
- [Polynomial equations for rational conformal field theories](/papers/1988-moore/) by Moore and Seiberg, 1988.

## Lectures

- [Lectures on RCFT](/papers/1989-moore4/) A lecture note reviewing the main results in the above papers. It is a very good resource for learning about the Moore-Seiberg construction of RCFT. It also presents CS/WZW duality as explicit examples of the Moore-Seiberg construction.


## Mathematical Perspective

Moore Seiberg construction has a deep underlying mathematical structure, which is modular functor and modular tensor category. It is also deeply related to the mathematical structure of TQFT. A mathematical review of these topics is given by: 

- [Lectures on Tensor Categories and Modular Functors](/papers/0000-bakalov/)
