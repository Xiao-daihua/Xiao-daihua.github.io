## Moore Seiberg Construction of RCFT

A series of extremely important and inspiring papers by Moore and Seiberg reveals a deep mathematical structure underlying rational conformal field theories (RCFTs). 

The main idea is that the conformal blocks of a RCFT forms a vector space, and represent the so called Moore-Seiberg groupoid. These conformal blocks satisfy a set of duality relations and the duality matircies satisfy a set of consistency conditions. 

By solving the consistency conditions, one can determine the duality matrices, which characterize a lot of information in RCFT. 

Moreover, the conformal blocks also give rise to a mathematical structure called modular tensor category, which is a very important mathematical structure in the study of TQFT, topological order ...
