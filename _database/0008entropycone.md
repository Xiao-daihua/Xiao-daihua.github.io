The Ryu-Takayanagi formula [[Ryu-Takayanagi Formula]] provides a geometric way to compute the entanglement entropy of the boundary region in holographic theories. This gives us some restrictions on the possible entanglement structure of the holographic states. The holographic entropy cone (HEC) is the set of all possible entanglement entropy vectors that can be realized by holographic states. It is known that the HEC is a convex cone and can be described by a set of linear inequalities.
