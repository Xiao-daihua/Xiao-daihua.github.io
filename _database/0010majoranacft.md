Majorana CFT (aka. free fermion CFT) is one of the simplest CFT and one of the first examples in textbooks. Yet it not quite correctly understood till recently.

## Tranditional Understanding 

Most of the traditional understanding of Majorana CFT mixed with its bosonization counterpart, which is the Ising CFT. However, one should understand these two as distict theories. 

Most structure of Majorana CFT can be found in the standart textbook: 
- [Applied Conformal Field Theory](/papers/0000-ginsparg/)
