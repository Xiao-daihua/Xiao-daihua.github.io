## Virasoro TQFT

Virasoro TQFT is a non-conventional topological quantum field theory that helps to calculate (in fact sort of defines) the gravitational path integral of 2+1 dimensional gravity on a topology fixed manifold with negative cosmological constant.

- [Solving 3d Gravity with Virasoro TQFT](/papers/2023-10eberhardt/) The original paper that proposed the Virasoro TQFT and explained how to use it to compute the gravitational path integral.

- [3d Gravity from Virasoro TQFT: Holography, Wormholes and Knots](/papers/2024-collier/) A follow-up paper that further explores the Virasoro TQFT.

## Liouville Conformal Blocks

The Virasoro TQFT can be view as an analogue of the Chern-Simons / WZW correspondence for Liouville CFT. The idea of using Liouville conformal blocks as a Hilbert space was first proposed by Herman Verlinde. 

- [Conformal Field Theory, Two-Dimensional Quantum Gravity and Quantization of Teichmüller Space](/papers/1990-verlinde/) Verlinde's original paper on the idea of using Liouville conformal blocks as a Hilbert space.
