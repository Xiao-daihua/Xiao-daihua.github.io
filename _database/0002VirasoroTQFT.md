Virasoro TQFT is a non-conventional topological quantum field theory that helps to calculate (in fact sort of defines) the gravitational path integral of 2+1 dimensional gravity on a topology fixed manifold with negative cosmological constant.

- [Solving 3d Gravity with Virasoro TQFT](/papers/2023-10eberhardt/) The original paper that proposed the Virasoro TQFT and explained how to use it to compute the gravitational path integral.
